The Process

Industrial waste,
designed material.

Reka Ruang started with a simple observation: enormous quantities of high-quality industrial material — ceramics, glass, mineral aggregate — leave Indonesian factories as waste every day. We built a process to reclaim them.

Each tile begins with sourcing, moves through our upcycling and composition process, and ends as a premium interior material with a documented material story that specifiers can stand behind.
