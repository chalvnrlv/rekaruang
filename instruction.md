# Project Instructions — Reka Ruang Website

## 1. Project Overview
Build a **company profile and product showcase website** for **Reka Ruang**, a business producing upcycled interior material products (notably the "Reka Tiles" collection). This is **Phase 1**: a branding, digital catalog, and lead-generation site — **no e-commerce or online payment** functionality is required at this stage.

## 2. Business Context
Reka Ruang produces interior materials made through upcycling processes. The website's core purpose is to support **brand positioning** and **business promotion**, presenting the company as a credible, sustainability-driven material supplier for the design and construction industry.

## 3. Target Audience
- Architects
- Interior designers
- Contractors
- Prospective project clients (B2B and B2C decision-makers evaluating materials for a project)

These users will primarily be evaluating Reka Ruang as a **material/product source**, so product clarity (specs, application, visuals) matters more than entertainment value.

## 4. Site Objectives
1. Communicate what Reka Ruang is and why its materials are different (upcycled, sustainable).
2. Showcase products clearly enough that a designer/contractor can shortlist them for a project.
3. Build trust through company story, mission, and sustainability commitment.
4. Generate leads via contact channels (WhatsApp, email, Instagram, contact form).
5. Work well on both mobile and desktop, since site visitors may browse on-site at a project location via phone.

## 5. Required Pages & Features

### 5.1 Homepage
- Hero section introducing Reka Ruang (who they are, what they do, one-line positioning).
- Value proposition section: why Reka Ruang's materials matter (sustainability, upcycling story, quality/design benefits).
- Visual showcase of products/materials (imagery-led, not text-heavy).

### 5.2 About Us
- Short company story / founding narrative.
- Vision and mission statement.
- Sustainability commitment section — explain the upcycling process and its environmental impact in a way that builds credibility.

### 5.3 Product Catalog
- Display the **Reka Tiles** collection (and be structured so future product lines can be added).
- Product photography for each item.
- Per-product details: material composition, available sizes/dimensions, and recommended applications (e.g., wall cladding, flooring, furniture surfaces).

### 5.4 Project / Application Gallery
- Real-world examples of the material applied in interior spaces.
- Gallery of completed projects and/or design visualizations, to help buyers picture the material in context.

### 5.5 Contact
- WhatsApp link/button (click-to-chat).
- Email address.
- Instagram link.
- Contact form (capture at minimum: name, email/phone, message — this is the primary lead-generation mechanism, so keep friction low).

### 5.6 Responsive / Mobile Behavior
- Fully responsive across mobile phones, tablets, and laptops/desktops.
- Product images and galleries must remain legible and fast-loading on mobile connections.

## 6. Explicitly Out of Scope (Phase 1)
- E-commerce functionality
- Online/in-site payment processing
- User accounts / login

## 7. Related Project Files

- **`SKILL.md`** — technical/agent execution guidelines (stack, conventions, workflow constraints).

## 8. Success Criteria
A visiting architect, interior designer, or contractor should, within a few minutes on the site, be able to:
- Understand what Reka Ruang is and its sustainability angle.
- Browse the Reka Tiles catalog with enough detail to evaluate fit for a project.
- See the material applied in real/visualized interiors.
- Reach out via WhatsApp, email, Instagram, or the contact form with minimal friction.
